          How To Play
Goal
	the goal of this game is to get to the end as fast as you can.
	in order to do that you will have to shoot blocks to get through 
	the walls in your way as well as jump across a gap.

Difficulty
	there are 3 difficulties which will determine how long you have 
	to make it to the end
		Easy      100
		Medium   75
		Hard        50

Movement
    use 'wasd' to move forward, left, back, and right
	use the mouse to look around
	press 'space' to jump

Pause
	press 'p' or '\' to pause the game

Shooting
	to shoot at the walls blocking your path 
		press the  left 'ctrl'
					or
		left click on the mouse
